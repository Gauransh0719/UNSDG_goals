UNSDG Goals Information

GOAL 1: NO POVERTY

End poverty in all its forms everywhere.
Goal 1 aims to eradicate extreme poverty by 2030 by focusing on the most vulnerable populations and providing access to basic resources and services.

GOAL 2: ZERO HUNGER

End hunger, achieve food security and improved nutrition, and promote sustainable agriculture.
Goal 2 aims to ensure access to nutritious food for all people, especially the most vulnerable, and promote sustainable agricultural practices.

GOAL 3: GOOD HEALTH AND WELL-BEING

Ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages.
Goal 3 aims to reduce maternal and child mortality, combat communicable diseases, and ensure access to essential health services.

GOAL 4: QUALITY EDUCATION

Ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all.
Goal 4 focuses on providing access to quality education, enhancing educational opportunities, and promoting lifelong learning for all.

GOAL 5: GENDER EQUALITY

Achieve gender equality and empower all women and girls.
Goal 5 aims to eliminate discrimination, violence, and harmful practices against women and girls and ensure equal opportunities in all spheres of life.

GOAL 6: CLEAN WATER AND SANITATION

Ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all.
Goal 6 focuses on ensuring universal access to clean water and sanitation, promoting water efficiency, and protecting freshwater ecosystems.

GOAL 7: AFFORDABLE AND CLEAN ENERGY

Ensure access to affordable, reliable, sustainable, and modern energy for all.
Goal 7 aims to increase the share of renewable energy in the global energy mix and improve energy efficiency to address climate change and promote sustainable development.

GOAL 8: DECENT WORK AND ECONOMIC GROWTH

Promote sustained, inclusive, and sustainable economic growth, full and productive employment, and decent work for all.
Goal 8 focuses on creating jobs, improving labor rights, and fostering economic growth that benefits everyone, particularly marginalized groups.

GOAL 9: INDUSTRY, INNOVATION AND INFRASTRUCTURE

Build resilient infrastructure, promote inclusive and sustainable industrialization, and foster innovation.
Goal 9 aims to enhance infrastructure, foster innovation, and promote sustainable industrialization to support economic development and address social and environmental challenges.

GOAL 10: REDUCED INEQUALITIES

Reduce inequality within and among countries.
Goal 10 seeks to reduce income inequality, promote social inclusion, and ensure equal opportunities for all, regardless of age, sex, disability, race, ethnicity, origin, religion, or economic or other status.
